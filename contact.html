FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping options?

We offer free standard shipping on orders over 500 THB. Express shipping (1-2 days) is available for 99 THB.

Can I return a product?

Yes! We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for unopened products. Opened items can be exchanged.

Are your products cruelty-free?

Absolutely. All GlowBeauty products are 100% cruelty-free and never tested on animals.

How do I track my order?

Once shipped, you'll receive a tracking email with a link to follow your package in real-time.
